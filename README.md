# DiscoveryAI

## AI-Powered Sales Discovery Engine

DiscoveryAI helps salespeople ask the right question before recommending the right product.

Most AI sales assistants focus on answering questions. DiscoveryAI focuses on discovering needs. Instead of immediately recommending a product, it identifies missing information, guides the salesperson toward the next best question, and improves the quality of the final recommendation.

## Why DiscoveryAI?

Most recommendation systems follow a simple workflow:

```text
Customer Need -> Product Recommendation
```

In real sales conversations, customer needs are often vague:

- "I need a laptop."
- "I want a Garmin watch."
- "I'm looking for a TV."

Good recommendations depend on how well the salesperson uncovers the customer's goals, constraints, budget, preferences, and hidden requirements.

Experienced salespeople know that the most important skill is not simply product knowledge. The most important skill is knowing what to ask next. DiscoveryAI is designed to make that skill easier to learn, repeat, and scale across sales teams.

## Product Vision

DiscoveryAI aims to become an AI Sales Coach that helps every salesperson perform more like a top salesperson.

It does not replace salespeople. It augments their ability to:

- Discover customer needs
- Ask better follow-up questions
- Handle uncertainty in conversations
- Improve recommendation accuracy
- Learn structured sales thinking

## How It Works

DiscoveryAI introduces a discovery-first recommendation framework:

```text
Customer Request
    ->
Discovery Engine
    ->
Information Gap Analysis
    ->
Next Best Question
    ->
Customer Response
    ->
Recommendation Engine
    ->
Product Recommendation
```

Instead of optimizing for the fastest recommendation, DiscoveryAI optimizes for the highest-quality recommendation.

## Example Workflow

### Customer

> I want a Garmin watch.

### Traditional AI Assistant

> Here are the top Garmin watches.

### DiscoveryAI

> Before recommending a model, ask:
>
> 1. What activities will you primarily use it for?
> 2. Do you need navigation features?
> 3. What is your budget range?

### Customer

> Running, swimming, budget around $800.

### DiscoveryAI

> Recommended product: Garmin Forerunner 265
>
> Reason: It fits running and swimming use cases while staying within the customer's stated budget range.

## MVP Scope

The first version focuses on one key question:

> Can AI identify the next best question a salesperson should ask?

Planned MVP features:

- Discovery question generator

- Product discovery trees
- Recommendation engine
